\chapter*{Use of Artificial Intelligence Tools}
\label{chap:ai-disclosure}

Artificial intelligence tools were used in the preparation of this thesis in the following limited capacities. The Overleaf built-in AI assistant was used for \LaTeX{} formatting and typesetting, proofreading of written text for grammar and clarity, and improving the flow and structure of written prose. A personal custom-built AI agent specialised in technical diagramming was used to generate schematic diagrams in the methodology sections. All experimental results, data analysis, and results figures were produced entirely by the author without AI assistance. The intellectual content, research direction, experimental design, and conclusions of this thesis are solely the author's own.
